What is a Beaver Tail?

A Beaver Tail (Queue de Castor in French) is a beloved Canadian fried dough pastry that resembles a beaver's tail. This delicious treat is stretched to a large, flat, oval shape and cooked until golden and crispy on the outside, while remaining soft and tender on the inside.

Originally created in Ottawa in 1978, Beaver Tails have become a Canadian icon and are now enjoyed across the country, including right here in beautiful Montreal!
